Final Project

The assignment

The final project is to make something big with the CNC, mold an object and cast then cast it and finally design and fabricate a model (30cm2) and produce a fiber composite of it. The group has to work together to document it, make a video where the project is summarized and make a BOM (Bill of materials) for the project.

Inspiration

For our final project we were going to make a beautiful epoxy table out of oak. Sadly because of Covid-19 we will not be able to finish the table this semester but we have made a detailed description on how we would do it. This photo found on Google is our inspiration for the epoxy table we are going to make.

The project

The team met in March and decided what we wanted to do for the final prjoect. Máni came with the idea to make a beautiful epoxy table out of oak and we went with that. It was made to look like the waterfall Hjálparfoss in Þjórsárdalur. Following is a picture of Hjálparfoss and the design made in Inventor.

I was in charge of the website. I chose a layout on the website https://html5up.net/. On the project website is a detailed documentation of how the table was made. I chose an appropriate licensing and uploaded it on the project website and made a bill of materials that shows what material we need to be able to make the table and in what quantity.

Here is the final project website.
